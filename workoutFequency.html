Workout Frequency

I hit each bodypart once and sometimes twice a week with moderate intensity. I'd say alternate doing one week twice and the next each bodypart twice. For example on Pull day train Back/ Biceps/ Hamstrings. Then on Push day, Chest/Triceps/ Side Delts/ Quads/calfs. Thats pretty much all the strength training basics, keep it simple. Train the upper and lower body, leave cardio, stretching and abdominal work for seperate days.
